Introduction:

The scripts contained in rosetta_windowing allow you to reproduce the energy analysis in Morris et al 2019 NSMB, using rosetta, they were written by Richard Sessions (Bristol University).

The scripts contained in rosetta_plotting allow you to reproduce the plotting and chimera visualisation of the energy analysis results. The scripts are designed to parse the rosetta analysis into a format comparible to the BUDE energy analysis, they were written by Kyle Morris (Warwick University).

Rosetta version x.x was used throughout
